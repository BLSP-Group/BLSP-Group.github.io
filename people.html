姜志国   Zhiguo Jiang

Prof. jiangzg@buaa.edu.cn

>>Professor Zhiguo Jiang received BE, MS and PhD degrees from Beihang University, Beijing, China, in 1987, 1990 and 2005, respectively. He subsequently worked as a teaching assistant at School of Astronautics, Beihang University. He was appointed Lecturer, Deputy Professor and Professor in image processing and pattern recognition in 1992, 1996 and 2005, respectively.
>>Professor Jiang's current research interests include Remote Sensing Image Analysis, Medical Imaging and analysis; Target classification, detection, and recognition.
