Content-based histological image retrieval

We introduce the content-based image retrieval (CBIR) apporach into histological image databases. It helps pathologicals analyze a region of histological image by searching for similar regions from a database containing diagnosed whole slide images. In this research involves studies on color normalization, feature extraction, hashing methods for histological images, and specific retrieval schemes from databases that consist of whole slide images. This research is supported by National Natural Science Foundations of China (No. 61371134 and No. 61771031) and Motic-BUAA Image Technology Research and Development Center.
